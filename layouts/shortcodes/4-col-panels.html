More Agile

Deepgreen runs TPCH up to 5X faster than Greenplum. With the speed gain, you can do more on existing cluster. In addition, Deepgreen supports zero-downtime full and incremental synchronization of the database to another cluster for DR, or additional compute.

More Versatile

Connect effortlessly to cloud storage and data source, including HDFS, S3, Oracle, Geode and Elastic Search. Imagine the relief when the cumbersome fact tables are, in fact, stored in HDFS or S3, and your DW becomes almost weightless.

More Intelligent

Tight integration with Tensorflow allows for high-bandwidth ML training, and enables in-database infererence with SQL.

Switch Effortlessly

Deepgreen DB is binary compatible with Greenplum. Switching from Greenplum to Deepgreen is as easy as 1-2-3:
  1. shutdown Greenplum,
  2. swap binaries,
  3. bring up Deepgreen.
